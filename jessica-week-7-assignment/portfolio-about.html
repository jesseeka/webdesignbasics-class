About Me

As a minimalistic designer,

I wish to express myself through simplicity and order.

Using asymmetrical shapes and specific color palette,

I like to develop a style unique to the piece/collection.

For me,

Design creates a natural and original mood. It communicates using color, texture, and attitude.

Design is timeless and visually expressive. It conveys a message.

My process,

Begins with inspiration from personal experience and observation of my surroundings.

However; through research and more exploration, I often find myself doubting.

I like to experiment with different textiles and create contrasting materiality.

While it could be a complicated process, I aim to keep the outcome simple.

After finding the determination to push through,

I wish to end up with a piece that conveys my point of view.

It is important to me,

That I challenge myself with each design,

And learn from my past failures and successes.
